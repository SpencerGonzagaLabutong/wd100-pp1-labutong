Bohol Bee Farm

Indulge in organic and sustainable farm-to-table dining at Bohol Bee Farm. Sample delicious dishes made with fresh ingredients harvested from the farm, including their famous flower salad, homemade ice cream, and artisanal cheese.

Loboc River Floating Restaurants

Experience a unique dining experience aboard a floating restaurant while cruising along the scenic Loboc River. Enjoy a buffet of Filipino specialties as you soak in the picturesque views of the surrounding lush forest and limestone cliffs.

Night Market in Tagbilaran

Explore the vibrant night market in Tagbilaran City, where you can feast on an array of local street food delicacies. Try grilled seafood, barbecue skewers, freshly fried lumpia (spring rolls), and other Filipino snacks while soaking up the lively atmosphere.
