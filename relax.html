Hinagdanan Cave

Escape the hustle and bustle of the city and unwind in the tranquil surroundings of Hinagdanan Cave. Marvel at the stalactites and stalagmites formations inside the cave, and take a refreshing dip in the clear blue waters of the underground pool.

Amorita Resort Spa

Treat yourself to a pampering spa day at Amorita Resort Spa, nestled atop a cliff overlooking Alona Beach. Choose from a variety of relaxing massages, body treatments, and facials designed to rejuvenate your mind, body, and spirit.

Balicasag Island

Embark on a boat trip to Balicasag Island, known for its pristine beaches and excellent snorkeling spots. Spend the day lounging on the white sand beach, swimming in the turquoise waters, or snorkeling alongside colorful coral reefs teeming with marine life.
