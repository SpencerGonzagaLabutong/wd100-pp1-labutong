CHOCOLATE HILLS

Explore the iconic Chocolate Hills, a geological formation consisting of at least 1,260 hills spread over an area of more than 50 square kilometers. Enjoy breathtaking views from the observation decks and take in the natural beauty of this unique landscape.

Tarsier Sanctuary

Visit the Tarsier Conservation Area to see the world's smallest primate, the Philippine tarsier, in its natural habitat. Learn about these adorable creatures and their conservation while walking through the sanctuary's lush forest.

Panglao Island

Head to Panglao Island for its stunning beaches and crystal-clear waters. Spend a day relaxing on Alona Beach, snorkeling among vibrant coral reefs, or diving to explore underwater caves and marine life.
